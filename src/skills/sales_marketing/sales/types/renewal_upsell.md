# Renewal + Upsell Presentation

**What it is:** A structured meeting deck for a contract renewal conversation — reviewing value delivered, securing the renewal commitment, and expanding scope with an additional product or seats.

---

## Audience
VP People, VP Operations, or equivalent business owner. They own the contract renewal. May have a competitive option on the table. They need to justify the spend internally and feel good about the decision.

## Tone
Confident and value-focused. Don't be apologetic about the renewal ask. If the product has delivered value, show it. Make the upsell a natural extension of the value story — not a surprise pivot.

---

## Slide Structure

1. **Cover** — Your company name, customer name, renewal period, date
2. **Partnership Timeline** — When they started, milestones, growth of the relationship.
3. **Value Delivered: By the Numbers** — Primary metrics vs. baseline. Year-over-year improvement.
4. **Usage + Adoption** — Active users, feature utilization, engagement trend.
5. **Customer Outcomes** — 2–3 named business outcomes achieved. Specific.
6. **What You've Said About Us** — NPS, quotes, or support ticket trends. Social proof from their own feedback.
7. **Roadmap: What's Coming for You** — Relevant upcoming features tied to their stated priorities.
8. **The Renewal Proposal** — Terms, pricing, duration. State it directly.
9. **Expansion Opportunity** — New module, seats, or use case. Lead with the problem it solves.
10. **Expansion ROI** — What the expansion investment returns. Model it simply.
11. **Why Renew and Expand Now** — Any incentive for committing early. Multi-year pricing, feature access.
12. **Proposed Next Steps** — Renewal signature timeline, expansion evaluation path.

---

## Must-Have Elements
- Value delivered with hard numbers — not just "we've been a great partner"
- Adoption data that shows the product is genuinely used
- Renewal terms stated clearly and early
- Expansion opportunity framed as solving a specific named problem
- A reason to decide now (early renewal discount, feature access, capacity planning)
- Competitive threat addressed proactively if known

## Must-Avoid
- Renewal meetings that skip the value review and go straight to pricing
- Upsell pitches that appear before the renewal is secure
- Value metrics that aren't relevant to the buyer's stated goals
- Leaving the competitive threat unacknowledged if you know it's there
- Generic "here's what's coming" roadmap slides with no connection to their needs

---

## Key Data Points to Include
- Adoption rate: % of eligible users active
- Primary success metric: before/after (or vs. target)
- NPS score (if tracked)
- Contract end date
- Renewal ARR and any change from prior year
- Upsell: module name, additional ARR, projected benefit

1. Lattice × [Company] — Renewal + What's Next

4. How Your Team Uses Lattice Today

7. 2025–2026 Renewal: Terms and Pricing
8. One More Step: The Compensation Module
9. Why Compensation Fits Now: Pay Equity Risk + Retention Context
10. Expansion ROI: $22K for $180K Retention Benefit
11. Renew + Expand: Multi-Year Pricing Available Through [Date]
12. Next Steps: Renewal Sign-Off + Compensation Pilot Decision
